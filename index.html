CivicConnect

Version 2.1.0

CivicConnect is a community-driven platform that empowers citizens to report, track, and resolve civic issues in their neighborhoods.

Our Mission

To create transparent, efficient communication between citizens and local authorities, making communities better places to live.

Features

  • Real-time issue reporting with photo evidence
  • Interactive map integration
  • Transparent status tracking
  • Push notifications
  • Public dashboard for community awareness

Tips for Effective Reporting

  • Take clear, well-lit photos of the issue
  • Be specific about the location
  • Provide detailed descriptions
  • Check similar existing reports first
  • Follow up if issue isn't resolved in timely manner
